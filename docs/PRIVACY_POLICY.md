# Chaos Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 18, 2026

Chaos is a fun soundboard app for recording, playing, and triggering short sounds.

## Data Stored On Your Device

Chaos may store the following on your device:

- Audio recordings you create in the app
- Recording names
- Favorite sounds
- App settings, including trigger sensitivity and theme accent
- Local play statistics
- Purchase unlock status

This data is stored locally on your device. Chaos does not operate a developer server that receives your recordings, favorites, settings, or play statistics.

## Permissions

Chaos may request these Android permissions:

- Microphone: used to record sounds and detect clap triggers.
- Notifications: used when background trigger controls are active.
- Sensors: used for shake, slap, and light trigger features.
- Wake lock and boot completed: used to support optional background trigger behavior and scheduled chaos alarms.

## Purchases

Chaos may use Google Play Billing to process optional in-app purchases. Purchase processing is handled by Google Play.

## Sharing

If you choose to share a recording, Chaos opens the Android share sheet. You control which app receives the shared file.

## Children

Chaos is intended for general fun use. Do not record or share private information through the app.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer listed on the Chaos Google Play store listing.
